Who we are

Wheat Capital Management, LLC. (WCM) is comprised of top-tier individuals from real estate, capital raising, technology, marketing and entrepreneurial sectors. The synergy of the team allows for a proactive approach in selecting investments that will be successful within the development of niche markets.

Operational Expertise

Joseph D. Touizer

Founder, Chief Executive Officer, Managing Partner

As Founder, CEO and President, Mr. Touizer utilizes two decades of entrepreneurship knowledge to Wheat Capital Management. The firm was built on Joseph’s personal passion of real estate alongside his venture capital and direct marketing experience. He has led four start-up companies within travel, computer, alternative investment and insurance industries. Joseph facilitates funding for various projects through his own networks of angel investors. His expertise in raising capital enables WCM to accelerate its leadership position in the evolving real estate investment industry. Joseph’s astute entrepreneurial skills and global vision create a new investment pathway towards residential and commercial real estate growth.

C.J. Maier

CPM, Executive Vice President, Managing Partner

As Executive Vice President, Mr. Maier brings his expertise in the real estate industry to acquisitions and business development for WCM. He carries over a decade of knowledge managing projects from initiation to realization. Most of his background includes directing multi-family and commercial properties including market rate, distressed, affordable and value-add properties. C.J. was most recently Vice President at Rivergate Real Estate for numerous projects focused in Florida. Coordinating construction, due diligence, market analysis or property management, C.J. thrives with a hands-on approach to attain maximum success. C.J. has served in a capacity of President for various Condo and HOA board of directors for new construction and conversion properties.

Alexandra Sanchez

Chief Strategy Officer, Managing Partner

As Chief Strategy Officer, Ms. Sanchez bridges strategy from structure and execution tactics. Alexandra seeks and develops business opportunities to accelerate top and bottom line revenue and increase shareholder value at WCM and its individual partnerships. Alexandra is a multi-faceted executive carrying over a decade of experience in direct marketing and global corporate branding. Her most recent work included consulting on direct marketing efforts for the LATAM markets in MasterCard’s Priceless campaign, B2B strategies for McCann World Group and market position strategies for a multi-national alternative investment firm. Alexandra holds a Bachelor of Science dual degree in Advertising and International Business from Florida International University. She is from Sao Paulo, Brazil and speaks fluent Portuguese and Spanish.

Diana Fung

Head of Capital Raising, Global Markets

As Head of Capital Raising in Global Markets, Ms. Fung ensures the facilitation for international investors to participate in WCM’s limited partnerships. Our international efforts allow for a full-service concierge experience where Ms. Fung aims to optimize profit potential for investors. She heightens our firm’s skill sets by sharing extensive knowledge through her private wealth brokerage journey of working at Merrill Lynch, Morgan Stanley, Bank of America and Santander Group. Diana holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Finance from the University of Miami. She is fluent in Chinese, Italian and Spanish.

Shanna Morris

Corporate Controller

As Corporate Controller, Mrs. Morris oversees all corporate accounting and finance matters. She has performed an executive role for the last 15 years through both start-up and established organizations in the healthcare industry. Shanna is integral in establishing and maintaining billing systems and controls. She served as Controller and a member of senior management for Cinergy Health, where she helped shape the infrastructure that supported one of the most successful companies in the direct-to-consumer health insurance market. Shanna holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting.

David Dimlich

Corporate Counsel

As Corporate Counsel, Mr. Dimlich ensures regulatory compliance is met across corporate and partnership initiatives. David received his Bachelor of Arts degree from Dartmouth College and his Juris Doctor degree from the University of Cincinnati, College of Law. He has practiced law for over two decades, primarily in transactional and corporate areas. Partners

Security Counsel
